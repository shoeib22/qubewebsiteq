OUR JOURNEY

Eight Years of
Crafting Dreams.

2016

The Beginning

Founded with one mission: to bring cinema-grade experiences into the homes of Hyderabad. Our first project — a 5.1 home theatre in Banjara Hills — set the standard.

2018

Smart Home Entry

Expanded into full smart home automation, partnering with Crestron and Lutron to deliver seamless integration across entire residences.

2020

Dolby Atmos Pioneers

Became one of Hyderabad's first certified Dolby Atmos residential installers — bringing object-based audio to private homes.

2022

50 Projects Milestone

Crossed 50 completed projects. Refined our end-to-end methodology: design, installation, and lifetime support under one roof.

2024

100+ Homes & Beyond

Over a hundred extraordinary living spaces across Hyderabad. The journey continues — each project more ambitious than the last.
